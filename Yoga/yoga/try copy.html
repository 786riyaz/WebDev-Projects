Special Therapies

Complementary therapies to enhance your healing and wellness journey.

Tapping (EFT)

Emotional Freedom Technique that combines ancient acupressure with modern psychology.

  • Stress and anxiety relief
  • Emotional balance
  • Pain management
  • Performance enhancement

Dry Cupping

Traditional therapy using suction cups to relieve muscle tension and improve circulation.

  • Pain relief
  • Improved blood flow
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Promotes relaxation

Wet Cupping

A form of bloodletting used to remove "harmful" blood from the body.

  • Detoxification
  • Pain management
  • Improves circulation
  • Traditional healing

Dry Needling

Modern treatment technique that uses thin filiform needles to release muscle trigger points.

  • Myofascial pain relief
  • Improved range of motion
  • Reduces muscle tension
  • Accelerates healing
